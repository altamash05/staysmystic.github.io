About us


Mystic Stays is an organisation aspiring to impact lives towards a sustainable living and influence in a way to live in harmony with nature and all its beings. Mystic Stays was born from the idea that holidays should be enriching, meaningful and joyful occasions. We are based in the beautiful Kashmir. ​We are a small team of passionate travelers who are on a mission to make travel more sustainable. Travel is about experiencing life and we're here to help you do just that.

Our Vision / Our Mission


"Mystic Stays aspires towards self-reliant, sustainable and responsible communities living in harmony with the earth. "

"To mobilize people, resources and platforms to empower and generate opportunities for the youth, women, men and children."
